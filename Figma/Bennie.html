Modern European Restaurant in Fitzrovia
At Bennie, we take pride in offering a modern European and Mediterranean-inspired dining experience. Our menu features a variety of carefully crafted dishes, from fresh seafood like grilled octopus, calamari and king prawns to tender meats, including our perfectly cooked ribeye steak. Vegetarian and vegan guests can enjoy vibrant, seasonal plates such as aubergine or broccoli tempura and roasted vegetable platters. Every dish is made with the freshest ingredients and showcases the bold, natural flavours.
